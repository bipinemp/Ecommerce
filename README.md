## Features

#-> You can view all the products available and click the individual product to open it's specfic page , where you can add to cart the product. <br>
#-> You can increase the quantitiy of product and click on proceed to checkout to purchase after filling the form correctly and submitting you get the transaction id.
#-> You can also filter the products according to the type , price , rating.
#-> You can also search for particular product on search box on navbar.
